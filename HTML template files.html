F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Below you"ll find answers to the most common questions?

  • What is economic freedom?

    Economic freedom is the fundamental right of every human to control his or her own labor and property.In an economically free society,individuals are free to work,produce,consume,and invest in any way they please.

  • How do you measure economic freedom?

    We measure economic freedom based on 12 quantitative and qualitative factors,grouped into four board categories,or pillars of economic freedom: 1)Rule of law property rights(government integrity,judicial effectiveness) 2)Government size(government spending,tax burden,fiscal health) 3)Regulatory efficiency(business freedom,labor freedom,monetary freedom) 4)Open markets(trade freedom,investment freedom,financial freedom)

  • Which economy allows the most economic freedom?

    Singapore's economy

  • Which economy has the least freedom?

    North korea

  • Is India a free economy?

    No,India is considered a mixed economy

  • Which is the first growing economy in the world?

    India remains the fastest growing economy in the world
